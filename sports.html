Sport-Specific Modules

McSportng adapts its interface and features based on the selected sport, providing tailored statistics tracking and management tools.

Football (Soccer)

Player Statistics

  • Goals scored
  • Assists
  • Yellow and red cards
  • Minutes played
  • Shots on target
  • Pass completion rate
  • Tackles and interceptions

Match Features

  • Match event timeline (goals, substitutions, cards)
  • Formation builder (4-4-2, 4-3-3, etc.)
  • Starting XI and substitutes
  • Match result and score
  • Possession statistics

Team Management

  • Position assignments (GK, DEF, MID, FWD)
  • Jersey number management
  • Captain and vice-captain designation

Baseball

Batting Statistics

  • At-bats (AB)
  • Hits (H)
  • Home runs (HR)
  • Runs batted in (RBI)
  • Batting average (AVG)
  • On-base percentage (OBP)
  • Slugging percentage (SLG)

Pitching Statistics

  • Innings pitched (IP)
  • Earned run average (ERA)
  • Strikeouts (K)
  • Walks (BB)
  • Wins and losses
  • Saves
  • WHIP (Walks + Hits per Inning Pitched)

Fielding Statistics

  • Putouts (PO)
  • Assists (A)
  • Errors (E)
  • Fielding percentage
  • Double plays

Game Features

  • Inning-by-inning tracking
  • Lineup and batting order
  • Pitch count monitoring
  • Box score generation

Golf

Player Statistics

  • Handicap index
  • Average score
  • Fairways hit percentage
  • Greens in regulation (GIR)
  • Putts per round
  • Driving distance
  • Sand save percentage

Scorecard Management

  • Hole-by-hole scoring
  • Par tracking
  • Stroke recording
  • Course handicap calculation
  • Net and gross scores

Tee Time Booking

  • Schedule tee times
  • Group formation (2-4 players)
  • Course selection
  • Booking confirmation and reminders

Kitty System

  • Create betting pools for tournaments
  • Set buy-in amounts
  • Configure payout structure (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
  • Automatic winner calculation
  • Payout tracking and distribution
  • Kitty balance management

Club Structure

  • Golf clubs (not teams)
  • Member management
  • Tournament organization
  • Club championships

Adaptive Behavior

The platform automatically adapts based on the selected sport:
